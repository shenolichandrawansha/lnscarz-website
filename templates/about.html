About LNS CARz

Home About Us

Welcome to LNS CARz — Rev Up Your Ride! Sri Lanka's Trusted Car Partner

LNS CARz is your trusted partner in buying, modifying, and reselling vehicles — offering exceptional customer service. Specializing in top-notch vehicle sales, we cater to every car enthusiast's dream. We expertly match Sri Lankans with their ideal vehicles!

Quality Assurance

Every vehicle at LNS CARz is thoroughly inspected, repaired, and refurbished before being listed for sale. We guarantee quality.

Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees, no surprises. We believe in honest and transparent pricing for all our vehicles and services.

Expert Brokering

Our experienced team acts as trusted brokers, ensuring smooth and fair transactions between private buyers and sellers.

Our Mission

To provide Sri Lankans with access to quality, reliable, and affordable used vehicles through a transparent and customer-first buying, selling, and brokering experience. We believe every Sri Lankan deserves a vehicle they can trust at a price they can afford.

Our Vision

To become Sri Lanka's most trusted automotive marketplace — where every customer walks away satisfied. We strive to set the gold standard in used vehicle sales, with a reputation built on honesty, expertise, and genuine care for our customers.

Our Values

Integrity, transparency, and customer satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do. We never compromise on quality — every vehicle is inspected, every price is fair, and every customer is treated like family.
